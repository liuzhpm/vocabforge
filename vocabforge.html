📖 How It Works
▼
Mastery System
Every word starts at New with 0 points. Points move your words through four levels:
New
0 pts
Learning
1+ pts
Familiar
3+ pts
Mastered
5+ pts
Scoring
✓ Got It or correct quiz → +2 pts
✕ Still Learning or wrong quiz → −1 pt
💡 Show Chinese hint → −0.5 pts and Got It is locked
Tap to reveal definition → Got It is locked (you needed help)
True mastery = knowing the word without checking definition or translation.
Features
Flashcards — Tap card to reveal definition, then rate yourself. Use ↩ Undo if you mis-click.
Quiz — 5 random multiple-choice questions per category.
💡 Chinese Hint — Optional per-card button. Shows translation but costs −0.5 pts. Over-reliance is tracked by the AI coach.
🧠 AI Coach — After each session, analyzes your patterns and gives personalized memory tips.
+ New Words — AI generates fresh vocabulary weekly. Optionally specify a topic.
Progress — Your scores are saved automatically between sessions.
Daily Routine (5–10 min)
1. Pick a category and run through flashcards
2. Try not to use the Chinese hint — challenge yourself!
3. Take the quiz to test yourself
4. Review the AI Coach summary for tips and level-ups
5. Once a week, tap + New Words to refresh your vocab pool
💾 Your Vocab Pool Data
Progress saves in this browser automatically. Export to back up or move to another device.
🔑 AI Features
AI-powered features (+ New Words, Explore Scenes, AI Coach) need an Anthropic API key. Your key stays in this browser only — never sent anywhere else.
